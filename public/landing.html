Support

Frequently Asked Questions

What is Tier Pass and how does it work?
Tier Pass is our flagship service that helps traders become funded. We provide a proprietary Expert Advisor (EA) that you install on your MetaTrader 4 or 5 platform. The EA trades your prop firm challenge automatically with a 94% success rate. You maintain full access to your account — we never request your login credentials.
Which prop firms are supported?
We support 20+ leading prop firms including FTMO, FundedNext, FundingPips, FunderPro, Blueberry Funded, The5%ers, E8 Markets, FXIFY, Goat Funded Trader, OANDA Prop Trader, and many more. Any firm that allows Expert Advisors on MT4 or MT5 is compatible.
What happens if the challenge fails?
If the challenge fails due to our EA's performance, you receive a full refund of the Tier Pass service fee within 30 days of purchase. The guarantee is void if you change bot parameters, manually open or close trades, or fail to follow our operational instructions.
Do you need my account login credentials?
No — never. We do not access, request, or store your trading account credentials under any circumstances. The EA runs locally on your MetaTrader platform. Your account, your control. For broker sync in the dashboard, we use the investor (read-only) password only.
What is Tier Manage?
After passing your challenge, you can keep our EA active on your funded account to generate consistent profits. We offer three plans: Lite Funded (5% target), Starter Funded (9% target), and Pro Funded (15% target). Drawdown parameters are fully configurable to match your prop firm's rules.
What does TradesAlba include?
Standard plan includes unlimited daily signals, AI-powered analysis, 1 TradingView indicator, and 24/7 support. Pro plan adds 2 trading software tools for live accounts, 3 TradingView indicators, advanced analytics, and VIP priority support. Both available monthly or annually.
What is your refund policy?
Tier Pass: full refund within 30 days if the challenge fails due to our EA (conditions apply). Tier Manage: no refunds, cancel anytime. TradesAlba: 7-day money-back guarantee from the date of subscription, no questions asked.
What payment methods do you accept?
All payments are processed through Stripe, supporting credit/debit cards (Visa, Mastercard, Amex), Revolut, and Klarna. Cryptocurrency payments are coming soon. Tier Pass is a one-time payment; Tier Manage and TradesAlba are recurring subscriptions.
