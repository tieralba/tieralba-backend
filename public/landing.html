Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know.

What is Tier Pass?
Tier Pass is our challenge-passing service. We provide an Expert Advisor that connects to your MetaTrader platform and helps you pass your prop firm challenge safely and efficiently.
What is Tier Manage?
Tier Manage is our account management service. After you receive your funded account, our EA connects to it and manages trades with professional risk management. You maintain full access.
What is TradesAlba?
TradesAlba is our premium trading dashboard with live signals, trading journal, risk management tools, TradingView indicators, and broker integration for tracking your trading performance.
Do I need to share my password?
No. Our EA connects using your investor (read-only) password for monitoring, and the master password is never required. We install everything via a private Zoom session.
Which prop firms do you support?
We support any prop firm that allows Expert Advisors on MetaTrader 4 or 5. This includes most major prop firms in the industry.
What is your success rate?
We currently maintain a 94% pass success rate across all challenges. We focus on consistent, low-risk trading to maximize the probability of passing.
Can I cancel anytime?
Yes, all subscriptions can be cancelled at any time from your client dashboard. There are no long-term commitments or hidden fees.
