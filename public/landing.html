Frequently Asked Questions

What is Tier Pass?
+
Tier Pass is our challenge-passing service. Our Expert Advisor connects to your MetaTrader platform and helps you pass your prop firm challenge safely and efficiently.
What is Tier Manage?
+
Tier Manage is our account management service. After receiving your funded account, our EA manages trades with professional risk management. You maintain full access.
What is TradesAlba?
+
TradesAlba is our premium trading dashboard with live signals, journal, risk tools, TradingView indicators, and broker integration for tracking performance.
Do I need to share my password?
+
No. We use the investor (read-only) password. The master password is never required. Everything is installed via a private Zoom session.
Which prop firms are supported?
+
Any prop firm that allows Expert Advisors on MetaTrader 4 or 5. This includes most major firms in the industry.
What is your success rate?
+
We maintain a 94% pass rate. We use consistent, low-risk trading to maximize your probability of passing.
Can I cancel anytime?
+
Yes, all subscriptions can be cancelled from your dashboard. No commitments, no hidden fees.
