Refund Policy

Last Updated: March 27, 2025

This Refund & Challenge-Guarantee Policy ("Policy") applies to all Tier Alba products and services, including but not limited to Tier Manage, Tier Pass, and TradesAlba.

1. General Refund Policy

1.1 No-Refund Principle

Payments are generally non-refundable, except in the limited circumstances expressly described in this Policy.

1.2 Refund Request Deadline

All refund requests must be submitted within 30 calendar days from the date of purchase. No refunds will be available after this period.

2. Full Refund — Bot Not Installed

2.1 Installation Guarantee

If the bot is not installed on your account within 14 calendar days after payment, Tier Alba will issue a 100% refund.

2.2 Installation Confirmation

Installation is deemed complete once you receive confirmation that the bot is active on your agreed trading platform.

3. Challenge-Passing Commitment

3.1 Expected Timeframe

The average time to pass a challenge ranges from 7 to 21 trading days. Adverse market conditions may extend this period up to four weeks.

Important: Extended timeframes (up to four weeks) do not, by themselves, constitute valid grounds for a refund.

3.2 Commitment Void Conditions

This commitment becomes void if you:

  • Change bot parameters without prior written approval from Tier Alba
  • Manually open or close trades
  • Fail to follow operational instructions provided by Tier Alba

4. Refund for Strategy-Induced Losses

A refund is only possible if verifiable evidence demonstrates that a loss was caused directly by the bot's strategy while all Tier Alba instructions were correctly followed.

Tier Alba reserves the right to request full access to your account history and statements to verify compliance with all requirements.

5. Situations Where Refunds Are Not Granted

Refunds will NOT be granted in the following circumstances:

5.1 Market-Related Losses

  • Losses caused by economic news events
  • High market volatility
  • Abnormal slippage or execution issues

5.2 Client Intervention

  • Manual opening or closing of positions
  • Modifying bot settings without authorization
  • Any other interference with the account without Tier Alba's written approval

5.3 Prop Firm Actions

  • Actions taken by proprietary trading firms unrelated to the bot
  • Account suspension, rule changes, or account closure by the prop firm

5.4 Policy Violations

  • Any breach of Tier Alba usage rules
  • Violation of our Terms of Service
  • Non-compliance with challenge conditions

6. Payment Methods & Refund Processing

Accepted Payment Methods: Credit/debit card payments, Klarna, and cryptocurrency (where available).

Refund Channel: Approved refunds are credited only to the original payment method used for purchase.

Processing Time: Refunds are processed within 5–10 business days after approval.

7. How to Request a Refund

Email: refund@tieralba.com

Your refund request must include:

  • Order ID
  • Purchase date and payment method
  • Detailed reason for the request
  • Supporting evidence (screenshots, account statements, etc.) if claiming strategy-induced losses

Tier Alba will acknowledge receipt of your refund request within 3 calendar days.
